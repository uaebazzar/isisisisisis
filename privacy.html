Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Luncher respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Information We Collect

Luncher does not collect any personal or non-personal information from users. This includes names, emails, device information, or any usage data. We simply do not collect data.

Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, Luncher does not share, sell, or distribute any user information to third parties, advertisers, or anyone else.

Advertising

Luncher uses Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements. No personal information is shared with Google or any advertisers. Ads are shown without tracking users.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

Children’s Privacy

Luncher is not intended for children under 13. Since we do not collect any information, there is no personal data from children collected.

Data Safety Compliance

Luncher fully complies with Google Play’s Data Safety requirements. No personal or sensitive data is collected, stored, or shared, ensuring complete user privacy.
